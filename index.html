Conduct

. All participants must conduct themselves in a professional manner. Any behavior that is deemed inappropriate or offensive by the organizers will not be tolerated and will result in immediate expulsion from the event.

CLEANING


All participants are expected to maintain cleanliness and hygiene at the venue.

Liability


.The organizers will not be held responsible for any loss, damage, or injury to participants or their property during the Auto Expo.

Registration

 All participants including exhibitors, visitors, media personnel, and vendors must register themselves with the organizers before entering the event

Safety and Security


Safety of all participants is of utmost importance. All exhibitors and visitors must follow the safety guidelines and instructions given by the organizers. The organizers will take necessary steps to ensure the security of the event and participants..

Timings


The Auto Expo will have specific timings for the entry and exit of participants. The timings will be mentioned on the entry pass and should be strictly adhered to. No participant will be allowed to enter or exit the event outside the specified timings.

Sports Cars

A sports car is a car designed with an emphasis on dynamic performance, such as handling, acceleration, top speed, the thrill of driving and racing

Super Bikes


A sport bike is a motorcycle designed and optimized for speed, acceleration, braking, and cornering on asphalt concrete race tracks and roads.

Vintage Bikes

A vintage bike might be a motorcycle or a bicycle from the past. It is an “old bike” a “vehicle on 2 wheels from the past”. It is an oldtimer i.e. at least 40 years old.
